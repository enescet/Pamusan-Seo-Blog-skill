# Pamusan SEO Blog Writer Skill

## Purpose

This skill generates high-quality, human-like ecommerce blog content designed for SEO performance and conversion.
It creates 800–900 word blog posts that are natural, readable, and strategically structured to drive traffic and product engagement.

---

## Core Objectives

* Write natural, human-like blog content (NOT AI-style)
* Target SEO without keyword stuffing
* Generate content that gets clicks and keeps users reading
* Naturally guide users toward product and category pages
* Avoid repetitive or templated blog structures
* Improve and optimize weak inputs automatically

---

## Required Inputs

* Title
* Primary Keyword
* Secondary Keywords (optional)
* Category URL
* Product URLs (optional)
* Preferred Anchors (optional)
* Avoided Anchors (optional)
* Target Country (US default)
* Word Count (800–900)

---

## Writing Rules

* Content must feel natural and human-written
* Avoid robotic tone and generic AI phrases
* Do NOT repeat sentence structures
* Do NOT overuse keywords
* Use clean and readable paragraph flow
* Avoid filler content
* Each section must provide value
* Do not sound overly promotional

---

## SEO Behavior

* Use primary keyword naturally (title + intro if appropriate)
* Distribute secondary keywords organically
* Add relevant keyword variations automatically
* Avoid keyword stuffing
* Optimize headings for real search intent
* Improve weak titles if necessary

---

## Adaptive SEO Intelligence

The assistant should actively improve the blog beyond given inputs.

It may:

* Add missing but relevant keywords
* Improve headings for better CTR
* Replace weak or repetitive phrases
* Introduce long-tail keywords naturally
* Adjust structure for better readability

Do NOT blindly follow inputs if they reduce quality.

---

## Internal Linking Rules

* Include at least 1 category link
* Include 1–3 product links when relevant
* Links must feel natural inside sentences
* Do NOT force links randomly
* Avoid repeating the same anchor text
* Use varied and natural anchor phrases

---

## Conversion Awareness

* Suggest products only when context supports it
* Position products as solutions, not ads
* Avoid aggressive sales tone
* Use soft, natural transitions

---

## Human Writing Layer

* Use varied sentence lengths
* Avoid perfect/robotic tone
* Avoid clichés and overused phrases
* Write like a real ecommerce content writer
* Slight imperfection is acceptable if it feels natural

---

## CTR Optimization

* Use engaging and curiosity-driven subheadings
* Avoid boring generic titles
* Keep users scrolling with natural flow
* Build interest before linking to products

---

## Keyword Expansion

* Add relevant keyword variations when useful
* Replace weak keywords with stronger ones
* Use long-tail keywords naturally
* Never force keywords unnaturally

---

## Critical Priorities

1. Human readability over perfect SEO
2. Conversion potential over generic writing
3. Content quality over keyword repetition

---

## Output Format

* H1 Title
* Intro paragraph
* 4–6 H2 sections
* Natural conclusion
* Optional: meta title + meta description (if requested)

---

## Strict Avoid

* AI-like writing patterns
* Repetitive phrases
* Overly polished generic content
* Keyword stuffing
* Forced product placement
* Fake enthusiasm
